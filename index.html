Hybrid Battery Service Process

Diagnose. Repair. Verify.

A clean three-step process built for NiMH hybrid packs — no guesswork, no upsell.

Cell-level diagnostic
We scan and load-test every module in the pack to find the weak cells — not just the pack as a whole.
Targeted repair or replace
Swap the bad modules with tested, reconditioned cells — or drop in a full replacement pack if that's the better long-term call.
Road-test & warranty
We clear codes, re-balance the pack, and verify performance before we leave. Every repair is backed by a 3-month warranty.
